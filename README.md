# Eurofood Corp – End-to-End Sales & Customer Analytics

## Executive Summary

End-to-end data analytics project analyzing Eurofood Corp sales performance using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

The goal was to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights to support decision-making in sales, marketing, and customer strategy.

Key outcomes:
- Identified high-value customer segments using RFM analysis
- Highlighted the strategic importance of Members in revenue stability
- Revealed product-level profitability concentration
- Built an interactive dashboard for business monitoring

---

## Business Objective

Understand the key drivers of revenue and profitability across:
- Customers
- Products
- Loyalty program (Members vs Non-Members)
- Time-based sales trends

---

## Tools

- Python (Data cleaning & exploratory analysis)
- SQL (Advanced analysis & customer segmentation)
- Power BI (Interactive dashboards)

---

## Python Analysis

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (revenue, profit, customer behavior)
- Initial identification of key business trends

Insights:
- Members show more stable purchasing behavior
- Revenue distribution is relatively balanced across products
- Significant variability in customer value

---

## SQL Analysis

Advanced analytical layer including:
- Complex joins and aggregations
- Window functions and ranking
- Revenue segmentation
- RFM customer analysis

Insights:
- Clear segmentation into Best, Loyal, At Risk customers
- Identification of frequent and high-value customers
- Product performance and profitability ranking

---

## Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard is structured into 3 analytical views:

### Sales Overview
- Global performance tracking
- Revenue vs Profit trends
- Product category contribution
- Loyalty program impact

### Product Insights
- Product profitability analysis
- Revenue and margin comparison
- Best-performing products identification

### Customer Insights
- Customer segmentation analysis
- Members vs Non-Members comparison
- High-value customer identification

---

## Business Impact

- Improved understanding of customer segmentation
- Identification of key revenue drivers
- Optimization opportunities for product portfolio
- Strategic insights for loyalty program enhancement

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates a complete end-to-end data analytics workflow combining Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform raw data into actionable business intelligence.
